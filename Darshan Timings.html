Darshan timing⏲

this is a table of when darshans are open,how long they take and then getting closed.
darshan start time duration(in minutes) darshan closes
Mangal Arati 4:30am 70 mins 5:00am
Darshan Arati 7:00am 45 mins 7:45am
Dhup Arati 8:15am 335 mins 11:50am
Raj Bhoga Arati 12:30am 30 mins 1:00pm
Dhup Arati 2 4:20pm 210 mins 6:30 pm
Sandhya Arati 7:00pm 30 mins 7:30
Shayan Arati 9:00 30 mins 9:30pm

There is no darshan between 1.00 pm – 4.20 pm, except on Sundays/ Bank Holidays and festivals when darshan extends from 12.30 pm – 3.45pm. On major festival days arati and darshan times can change. Please check the festival schedule for confirmation.

Entrance Gates to Bhaktivedanta Manor are locked at 9:30pm.Remember that?
